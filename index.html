Quick and Tasty Hummus Recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400g) of chickpeas (garbanzo beans)
  • 175g of tahini
  • 6 sundried tomatoes
  • 1/2 a red pepper
  • a pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • a dash of olive oil

How to make the Hummus:

  1. Remove the skin from the clove of garlic, and chop coarsley.
  2. Grab the pepper and remove the seeds, chop in half, and continue to chop coarsley.
  3. Put all the ingredients into a food processor to make it into a paste.

  • If you would like a thicker hummus, process for a short time.
  • if you would like a smoother hummus, process until smooth.

Expirement!

If you would like to try and make different flavors of hummus for different dishes, here are some suggested ingredients:

  • lemon
  • coriander
  • chili pepper
  • lime and chipotle
  • harissa and mint
  • spinach and feta cheese

How to store the Hummus

The common way to store the hummus is in a sealed container in the fridge. The hummus should last about a week, but if it goes before then, please throw it out.

    -Hummus can also be stored in the freezer. Thaw it when you want to use it, and it should be used within a couple of months.
